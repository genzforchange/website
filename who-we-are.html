Who We Are

Gen-Z for Change leverages the power of social media, advocacy, and code to drive tangible progressive change. We regularly activate our coalition of hundreds of social media influencers on TikTok, Twitter, Instagram, and YouTube that reach more than half a billion followers collectively. We educate our audiences on issues that range from climate to reproductive justice and empower them with ways they can directly be part of the solution.
